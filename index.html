Our Story

Hi, I'm Lucy Irechukwu, the heart and hands behind LUCIANA HOME OF CAKES & PASTRIES.

My baking journey started in secondary school during Food and Nutrition class. What began as a simple passion quickly grew into a full business. I studied History and International Relations at Lagos State University, but my real calling has always been creating moments of sweetness.

Today, as a wife and mother to a cute son, I run Luciana Home of Cakes & Pastries from Lagos. We specialize in premium custom cakes, delicious pastries, and celebration treats that bring joy to families and events across the city.

Whether it's a birthday, wedding— our cake is made with love, the freshest ingredients, and that signature Luciana touch.

Location: NO 45D MBA STREET, OREGE, APAPA AJEGUNLE, LAGOS, LAGOS STATE (Apapa base with deliveries in Victoria Island & beyond)
Specialty: Custom cakes, pastries, small chops & celebration treats
Passion: Turning every bite into a beautiful memory ❤️
